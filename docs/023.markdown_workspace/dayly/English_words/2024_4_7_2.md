#   similar words :
summary: additional 附加的; extra 额外的; supplementary 补充的，补充性的。  
additional synonyms for extra .

*   `additional`:  
    名词 **additon派生** 出的形容词，指在 **原有基础上添加上去的**。  

    **Example**: additional resources/funds/security额外资源；附加基金；外加保安措施

*   `extra`·:  
    Beyond what is due,usual,expected,or necessary; extraneous 外来的，无关联的;additional;supernumerary 补充的.

*   `supplementary`:  
    由名词**supplement派生**而来，指对原有的追加或补究。

    **Example**: Supplementary oxygen is rarely needed in pressurized aircraft
    密封的飞机内很少需要补充氧气。
